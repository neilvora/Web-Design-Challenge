Humidity




As seen in the chart above, it appears that there may be a very minor correlation between Humidity and distance from the Equator. Looking purely at the data without a full regression analysis - there seems to be a cluster of high (100%) humidity locations near 0 Latitude and as we move further from this - there appears to be a very slight drop off in Humidity in both directions.
